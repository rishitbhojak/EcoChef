Frequently Asked Questions

How does Flavorish work?

Flavorish uses advanced AI algorithms to match the ingredients you have on hand with a vast database of recipes. You can get recipe suggestions based on what you have and follow step-by-step instructions to prepare your meals.

Is Flavorish free?

Flavorish offers a free version with basic features. For more advanced features, we offer Pro and Premium plans.

What is the difference between the Pro and Premium plans?

The Pro plan includes advanced recipe suggestions and nutritional information, while the Premium plan offers exclusive recipes and personalized meal plans in addition to all Pro features.

Can I use Flavorish on my mobile device?

Yes, Flavorish is available on both the App Store and Google Play. You can also use it on the web.

How do I sign up for Flavorish?

You can sign up for Flavorish on our website or through our mobile apps. Simply create an account and start exploring recipes tailored to your ingredients.
