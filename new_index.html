Frequently Asked Questions

How does EcoChef work?

EcoChef uses advanced algorithms to match the ingredients you have on hand with a vast database of recipes. You can get recipe suggestions based on what you have and follow step-by-step instructions to prepare your meals.

Is EcoChef free?

EcoChef is totally free for any user; just signup is required.

How EcoChef is innovative?

EcoChef significantly enhances the meal preparation process by allowing users to input specific dietary preferences, allergies, available preparation time, and nutritional targets.

Can I use EcoChef on my mobile device?

Yes, EcoChef is available on mobile device. Our website is responsive.

How do I sign up for EcoChef?

You can sign up for EcoChef on our website. Simply create an account and start exploring recipes tailored to your ingredients.
